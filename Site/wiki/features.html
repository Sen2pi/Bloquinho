Features

  • Rich Notes & Pages: Create unlimited pages with markdown, tables, code, images, and more. Link pages, use slash commands, and organize your knowledge.
  • Powerful Databases: Manage tables with 17+ data types, advanced filters, math operations, and inline editing. Integrates with agenda and kanban.
  • Agenda & Kanban: Visualize events, tasks, and deadlines. Drag & drop, calendar view, reminders, and kanban boards.
  • Password Manager: Store, analyze, and organize passwords with advanced security, 2FA, vaults, and breach detection. Works offline.
  • Documents & Cards: Manage IDs, loyalty cards, and credit cards with validation, search, and export.
  • Cloud Sync & Backup: Sync with Google Drive, OneDrive, or use offline. Full backup, restore, and export options.
  • Wiki System: Every HTML file in /wiki is a page. Easy to expand documentation and share knowledge.
  • Open Source & Free: MIT License, no ads, no tracking, no vendor lock-in. Community-driven and privacy-first.

Introduction

Bloquinho is a modern, open source, privacy-first workspace for notes, databases, tasks, and knowledge management. Designed for power users, students, and professionals, it offers a beautiful, cross-platform experience with advanced features and total data control.

  • 100% free and open source (MIT License)
  • Cross-platform: Windows, Linux, macOS, Web, Mobile
  • Modular, extensible, and easy to use
  • All data stored locally by default, with optional cloud sync
  • Advanced markdown, slash commands, databases, agenda, password manager, and more

Explore the wiki to learn more about each feature, security, architecture, and how to get the most out of Bloquinho!
